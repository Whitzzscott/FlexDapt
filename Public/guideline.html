Community Guidelines

Restricted Content
NSFW Content Policy:
- NSFW content is explicitly allowed on FlexDapt
- Exception: Real life/realistic NSFW images
- Verification challenges:
  • Age verification (18+)
  • Prevention of deepfaked content
  • Consent verification
- Only stylized, non-photorealistic NSFW content permitted
Prohibited Content:
- Self-harm or suicide-related content
- Gore or extreme violence
- Exploitation of tragedies/current events
- Content that promotes harmful behavior
Zero Tolerance for Discrimination:
Hate speech or discrimination based on:
- Race/Ethnicity
- Religion/Beliefs
- Gender Identity
- Nationality
- Sexual Orientation
- Disability
- Age
- Other protected characteristics
Exception: Actors in character roles only
Criminal Content:
- Glorification of criminals
- Content featuring violent offenders
- Promotion of illegal activities
- Terrorist/extremist content
- Cybercrime promotion
Minor Protection Policy:
Strictly prohibited:
- Underage characters (under 18)
- Child-like appearances
- Child-like mental states
- School-age implications
- Youth-targeted media
- Underage user content
ZERO TOLERANCE FOR MINOR-RELATED CONTENT
